Our Subjects

Our curriculum is built around the New Zealand Curriculum's eight learning areas, giving every student a broad and balanced education.

Science

Biology, Chemistry, and Physics explored through hands-on inquiry and NCEA pathways.

English

Critical reading, creative writing, and confident communication for every year level.

Mathematics

From algebra to calculus, building strong foundations for further study and everyday life.

Social Sciences

Geography, History, and Economics — understanding the world we live in.

Digital Technology

Programming, web design, and computational thinking for the modern world.

The Arts

Visual Art, Music, and Drama fostering creativity and self-expression.

Languages

Te Reo Māori, Spanish, and Mandarin supporting cultural understanding and connection.

Health & PE

Wellbeing, physical literacy, and healthy relationships across all year groups.

Technology

Materials, design, and innovation through practical project-based learning.
