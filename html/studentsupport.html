Student Support

We are committed to supporting every student's academic, social, and personal wellbeing

How We Support You

Whatever you need — academic, personal, spiritual, or career-related — there is someone here to help.

Pastoral Care

Every student belongs to a form class and is supported by a dedicated Form Teacher and Dean. Pastoral staff are available to help with any personal, social, or wellbeing concerns throughout the year.

Contact a Dean

Academic Support

Peer tutoring, subject clinics, and teacher-led study sessions are available throughout the year. Students can also access the Learning Centre for one-on-one support with any subject.

Find out more

Counselling

Our qualified school counsellors provide a confidential space for students dealing with stress, anxiety, or personal challenges. Referrals can be made through any staff member or self-referred.

Book an appointment

Careers Guidance

Subject selection, university applications, and career pathway planning are supported by our dedicated Careers Advisor from Year 10 onwards. Workshops and one-on-one sessions are available.

Meet our Careers Advisor

SENCO & Learning Needs

Students with specific learning needs are supported by our Special Education Needs Co-ordinator (SENCO), who works closely with families and teachers to put the right plans in place.

Talk to SENCO

Chaplaincy

Rooted in the Rosminian charism, our Chaplain is available to all students for spiritual support, reflection, and community service opportunities. Drop-in sessions are held weekly.

Connect with the Chaplain

Not sure who to talk to?

Reach out to us and we will connect you with the right person.
